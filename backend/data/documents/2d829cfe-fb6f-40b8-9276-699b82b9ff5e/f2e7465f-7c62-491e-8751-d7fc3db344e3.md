# MegaTrade — Company Policies (Demo)

## Customer Payment Terms Policy

All customer invoices are issued on **Net 30** terms: payment is due within 30
calendar days of the invoice date. Strategic accounts with an annual purchase
volume above 500,000 may be granted **Net 45** terms with CFO approval. Invoices
unpaid 15 days past their due date are flagged as **overdue** and routed to the
collections workflow. A late-payment surcharge of 1.5% per month applies to any
balance overdue by more than 30 days.

## Credit Policy

Every customer is assigned a **credit limit** recorded on their business-partner
master record. A customer whose outstanding balance exceeds their assigned credit
limit is considered **over-limit** and new sales orders for that customer require
manager approval before they can be released. New customers start with a default
credit limit of 50,000 for the first 90 days. Credit limit increases above
200,000 require Finance Director sign-off and a review of the last 12 months of
payment history.

## Return and Refund Policy

Goods may be returned within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund, provided
they are unused and in original packaging. Returns between 31 and 60 days are
eligible for store credit only. A **15% restocking fee** applies to returns of
special-order items. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within
**7 business days** of the returned goods being received and inspected. Items
marked as final-sale or clearance are not eligible for return.

## Procurement Approval Policy

Purchase orders up to 25,000 may be approved by a department manager. Purchase
orders between 25,000 and 100,000 require Procurement Lead approval. Any purchase
order above 100,000 requires both Procurement Lead and CFO approval before it is
sent to the supplier.
